OKRA SOUP RECIPE

Okra soup has origins in West Africa. It is believed to have originated from the Igbo people of Nigeria. The history of okra, the soup's main ingredient, traces back to East Africa, where it was cultivated by ancient Egyptians and spread through North Africa and the Middle East.

What You Should Know About Okra Soup

Did you know that Okra recipe is easy to follow, and also the Ingredients are quite easy to get.
The cook time is roughly around 30mins plus prep time at most, and it is best served with Garri otherwise known as EBA. Okra-benefits is countless.

Ingredients list

  • Fresh Okra
  • Palm Oil
  • Chili Peppers
  • Protein (Beef, fish, shrimps)
  • Seasoning
  • Onions
  • Locust Bean

Cooking Instructions

  1. Slice the okra and set it aside in a bowl
  2. Blend the chili Peppers
  3. Allow protien to boil together with all the Seasoning
  4. Introduce the palm oil after the protein is well cooked, and allow to boil
  5. Add the sliced okra and stir
  6. Leave to simmer for few minutes

Your soup is ready!
